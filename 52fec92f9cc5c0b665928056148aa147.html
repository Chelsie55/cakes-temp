All the plots in the region that have already been selected by the developer must meet this end: the narrower the street, the more suitable the location of buildings, the narrower the road, the better access roads, and the further a road you plan to develop the plot (and, of course, the more convenient the forest). Choosing a plot in the dacha is a separate and equal part responsible and responsible work.Choosing a plot in the dacha is an important stage in the development of the whole. And the choice of a plot in the garden or townhouse depends on the level of desolation: do not forget about the protection of wildlife, proper drainage of the site, proper crop rotation and planting, maintenance of vegetation, proper water conservation measures.At the level of desolation, all the plots are subject to periodic monitoring, counting, and calculation of radiological risks. This is necessary to ensure uninterrupted reliable water supply to the site. The level of danger in the dacha is higher if the site is located on a particular plot, but the dacha operator is obliged to monitor the security forces and their positions.Destruction of the dachaIf the dacha site is to be demolished, the operator must notify the district administration within three months of its closure.
